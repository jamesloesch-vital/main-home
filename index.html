What we treat

Low testosterone is a common condition that can be caused by a variety of factors, requiring a personalized approach. We treat both primary and secondary hypogonadism through a variety of treatments, helping you find the best solution for your unique needs.

Primary
hypogonadism

What it is

A condition where the testicles themselves don't produce enough testosterone.

Who it affects

Men with testicular injuries, certain genetic conditions, or those who've had radiation/chemotherapy. Also occurs naturally as men age, especially after 40, with significant increases in men over 60.

Treatment

Get effective testosterone replacement through our service with options like gels, patches, or injections to restore your levels to normal.

Secondary
hypogonadism

What it is

The testicles are healthy, but the brain isn't sending proper signals to make testosterone.

Who it affects

Most common in men 30-50, particularly those with obesity, diabetes, high stress, or those taking certain medications.

Treatment

Through our service, receive personalized treatments including lifestyle guidance, enclomiphene to boost your body's natural production, or testosterone replacement if needed.
